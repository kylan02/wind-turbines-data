Wind Turbine Data

This CORGIS Dataset I used is on all of the commercial wind power plants in the United States. Each wind turbine is recorded with many statistics about it including its power generated, height, turbine diameter, position, year it became operational, and many other facts. These statistics can be used to review the data and figure out how wind turbine efficiency has increased over time as well as other statistics we are able to measure.

According to the website that CORGIS got its data, the United States Wind Turbine Database, almost all of the wind turbines recorded are utility scale, which means that they generate power that is fed right into the grid.
